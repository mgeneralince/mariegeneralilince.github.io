About me

I am a PhD student in the Fairplay joint team at Inria Saclay under the supervision of Solenne Gaucher and Patrick Loiseau. My work focuses on algorithmic fairness.

Publications

Geometry of Relaxed Fair Regression: A Unified Framework for Aware and Unaware Settings
M. Generali Lince, V. Divol, R. Flamary, S. Gaucher, P. Loiseau. NeurIPS 2026. View PDF.

This paper proposes a unified framework for relaxed fair regression using optimal transport. It provides explicit solutions for both aware and unaware settings, along with an efficient post-processing algorithm.

Counterfactually Fair Regression via Optimal Transport
M. Generali Lince, S. Gaucher, J.-J. Vie, P. Loiseau. View PDF.

This work demonstrates that counterfactual fairness (CF) is equivalent to conditional demographic parity. It introduces a post-processing method with precise statistical bounds to control the accuracy-fairness trade-off.
